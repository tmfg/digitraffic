The Finnish Transport Infrastructure Agency (Väylävirasto) has discontinued publishing bridge and lock disruption data.

🔴 The `/api/bridge-lock/v1/disruptions` endpoint will be removed on 20.4.2026.

No replacement data source is available.

Supported and deprecated APIs are listed [here](/en/support/api-changes/).\
Swagger documentation: [meri.digitraffic.fi/swagger/](https://meri.digitraffic.fi/swagger/)
